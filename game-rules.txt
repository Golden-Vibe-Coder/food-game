Baxter's Burgers & Fries — Game Rules
======================================

HOW TO PLAY
-----------
- Customers arrive and wait in the queue with their order shown as icons
- Build the matching order by tapping the menu items at the bottom
- Tap the customer to serve them — if your order matches, you get paid
- Tap "Clear" to reset your current order and start over
- Customers change mood over time: 😊 (fresh) → 😐 (waiting) → 😠 (impatient)
- A customer leaves after 30 seconds if not served


MENU & EARNINGS
---------------
- 🍔 Burger        — $15
- 🍟 Fries         — $7
- 🥤 Milkshake     — $9
- 🥒 Fried Pickles — $3
- You earn the full item total when an order is served correctly
- Item order does not matter — just the items themselves


STORE RATING (0–5 stars)
------------------------
- Your rating goes up each time you correctly serve an order
- The faster you serve, the bigger the rating boost
  - Serving immediately: +1.0 stars
  - Serving near the deadline: +0.03 stars
- Your rating goes down when:
  - You serve the wrong order to a customer (-0.078 to -0.086 stars)
  - A customer leaves because their time ran out (-0.078 to -0.086 stars)
  - Larger orders penalize slightly less than smaller ones
- Rating can never go below 0 or above 5


TIPS
----
- Prioritize angry customers (😠) — they are about to walk out
- Serving quickly earns more rating than serving slowly
- A wrong order costs you rating, so double-check before tapping a customer
- Up to 4 customers can be waiting at once — keep the queue moving
